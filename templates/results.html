Vegetables — Largest Portion

Fill ~37% of your plate. Aim for 5-a-day (Eatwell). Use variety: spinach, bhindi, broccoli, carrot, tomato. Potatoes don't count.

🌾
Whole Grains — Quarter Plate

Whole wheat roti, brown rice, oats, daliya. Avoid maida and white rice. Choose higher-fibre versions (Eatwell).

🫘
Healthy Protein — Quarter Plate

Dal, rajma, chana, eggs, fish (2x/week per Eatwell), poultry. Limit red meat. Avoid processed meats entirely.

🍎
Fruits — Colourful Variety

Eat plenty of fruits of all colours. Limit juice to 150ml/day (Eatwell). Banana, papaya, apple, guava, orange.

🫒
Healthy Oils — Small Amounts

Mustard oil, olive oil, rice bran oil (unsaturated). Limit butter and ghee. Avoid trans fats / vanaspati.

💧
Water — 6–8 Glasses/Day

Water, unsweetened tea/coffee. Limit juice to 150ml/day. Avoid sugary drinks and sodas (Eatwell Guide).
